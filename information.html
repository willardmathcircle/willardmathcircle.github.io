What is a Math Circle?

​A math circle differs from a typical middle school math club in the intent. Instead of covering standard curriculum, a math circle explores branches of math that a formal education doesn't, immersing students into mathematics through puzzles, explorations, and collaboration. Competition preparation is often included, but math circles often ask students to think mathematically and develop strategies for tackling math at all levels. Most importantly, a math circle is a place for students to have fun and engage with math in a unique way!

Challenge Problem
